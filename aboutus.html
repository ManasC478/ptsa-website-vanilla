Fremont High School PTSA

The Fremont High PTSA (Parent-Teacher-Student-Association) is a non-profit organization dedicated to bring all the stakeholders in the Fremont High School community to make Fremont High School a place where our children will learn, grow and thrive. Fremont High PTSA is a volunteer-driven organization and is funded solely by donations from the community. Fremont High PTSA fund and support many programs that assists students, staff, teachers, and parents throughout the course of high school and beyond.

If you have questions please send an email to fremontptsa@gmail.com
